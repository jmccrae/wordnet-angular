Open English WordNet
Open English WordNet is derived from Princeton WordNet by the Open English WordNet Community and released under the Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY) 4.0 License. We welcome any corrections, improvements or other contributions at GitHub. A full list of contributors is available on GitHub.
This interface was created by John P. McCrae at the Data Science Institute, National University of Ireland Galway (GitHub).
